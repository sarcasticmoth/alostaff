[blockquote][blockquote][blockquote][div style="text-align:center;"][b][font size="5"]Hogwarts Classes[/font][/b][/div]
You do not have to use this claim for your students if you don't want to. This is so you can keep track of your classes, clubs, club positions, and quidditch positions.

4th and 5th years take all the core classes and a minimum of two electives.

6th and 7th years take a minimum of three core classes and a minimum of one elective.

Special classes are only offered for specific years, and are required by all students in that year.

[div style="text-align:center;"][u][b][font size="5"]To claim:

[/font][/b][/u][/div]List club/class and then fill out this code for each students you want to add. For clubs you must also specific you want the captain or co-captain position.&nbsp;

For Quidditch, you must list position.

class/club/position [/*code][/code]
P.S. Please keep your classes in order (cores together, electives together, in alphabetical order) to make it easy to add to the list!
