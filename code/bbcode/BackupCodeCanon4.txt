[table style="color:rgb(85, 85, 85);"][tbody][tr][td][div style="text-align:justify;"][/div]JK Rowling has supplied us with a plethora of surnames that exist in the magical world but we don't know much about.

These families are entirely up for grabs for characters. So, a few ground rules about them, to start:

[ul type="circle"][li]The first person to claim a surname is the 'creator' and gets to decide the basic family makeup. Parents, blood status (They do have to be half or pureblood. These are magical families so they can't be muggleborn.), siblings, history, etc.&nbsp;[/li]
[li]No direct descendants of characters we know died. You can make relatives of them though.[/li][/ul]

Please note: Purebloods are not common, and most wizards now a days are half bloods. So if you want a pureblood you have to take one of these names.  There are a few exceptions listed in the rules though!
